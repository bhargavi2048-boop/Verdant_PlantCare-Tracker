Our Story

About Verdant

Why we built a home for plant lovers everywhere

Born from a wilted monstera

Verdant started when its creator forgot to water a beloved Monstera for three weeks. After watching it recover slowly, we realised there had to be a better way — a simple, beautiful tool to help people remember, track, and truly enjoy caring for their plants.

We believe plants make life better. They clean the air, calm the mind, and bring a little nature indoors. But keeping them alive shouldn't feel stressful. Verdant makes it effortless.
